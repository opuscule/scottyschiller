Scott Schiller

Catalyst for Growth
Proven Commercial Leader
Mentor

Scott Schiller is an expert in business model pivot and transformation.

Schiller, a founder and former chairman of The Interactive Advertising Bureau, brings comprehensive expertise in content, media, and tech. He recently served as Global Chief Commercial Officer at Big Village, a New York-based ad-tech firm, and has taught at NYU Stern School of Business for over five years.

Schiller's career spans senior roles in Fortune 50 firms to startups, including a decade at Comcast, establishing NBCUniversal's digital video ad sales business.

He holds a BA in Economics from Cornell University and an MBA from Northwestern's Kellogg School of Management.

Adjunct Professor

Schiller is also an adjunct assistant professor at NYU/Stern, teaching "Technology's Impact on Entertainment and Media."

Schiller brings his hands-on experience to the entertainment business. He works with undergraduate and graduate students as a teacher, mentor, and advisor to student clubs and start-ups.
